**Table 1 | Mathematical specifications of 26 single-cell dimensionality-reduction methods.** Each row records features traceable to the published formulation, including the observation or input model, latent parameterisation, explicit objective terms and the mathematical object directly constrained during fitting. The final column identifies properties that are not established by those equations and therefore require empirical measurement. Methods may contain several objective terms; the rows are not mutually exclusive classes and do not constitute a performance ranking. The controlled vocabulary, row-level provenance and coding rules are provided in Supplementary Table S4, and full method-specific derivations are provided in Supplementary Note 1.
